About Us

Our story, mission and the team behind SynapSense

Our Story

We live in a world that wasn't designed for our minds or bodies—where sensory needs are dismissed, especially for older adults and the neurodivergent among us. This isn't just a product story. It's our story.

Born from the intersection of lived neurodivergent experience and professional occupational therapy insights, we've created what we needed but couldn't find: assistive sensory tech that allows us to live and age well with healthspan in mind.

Our tool stands against overstimulating environments, inaccessible therapies, and systems that weren't built with different but amazing brains in mind. We've transformed frustration into innovation—creating tech that's evidence-informed and intentionally inclusive from its inception.

We're reimagining sensory therapy with tech. Meeting you exactly where you are with the tech and therapy to support you—whether you're navigating the experience of dementia, autism, trauma, or simply aging on your own terms.

Because dignity isn't optional. Autonomy isn't a luxury. And lived experience integrated into design is necessary for innovative tools that work for us.

Our Team

Meet the founders behind SynapSense

Brenda Luo, Reg OT, MSc

Co-Founder

Brenda is an occupational therapist and innovation consultant who combines clinical expertise with strategic design to create human-centered healthcare solutions. At the intersection of rehabilitation practice and healthtech, she partners with organizations to develop practical tools that improve patient outcomes while growing rehabilitation innovation locally in Alberta.

Brenda's experience includes co-designing therapy technologies with diverse communities, developing inclusive research methodologies, and guiding teams through sustainable innovation processes. Her passion lies in transforming bold healthcare visions into tangible solutions that enhance rehab, mental health, and workplace wellness.
