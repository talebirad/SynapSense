Our Solution

AI-driven neuromechanical training for enhanced care

Neuromechanical Training Tool

Our AI-driven neuromechanical training tool is a multimodal prompting system designed to assist individuals in completing self-care tasks, pre-activity mobilization, and anxiety management in various care environments.

It leverages AI to deliver real-time, context-aware sensory cues — visual and auditory — tailored to the individual's motor, cognitive and sensory profile.

Key Benefits

Making a meaningful difference in care delivery

Our solution promotes long-term health and functionality by providing targeted sensory interventions that support cognitive and physical well-being throughout the aging process.

Empowering individuals with personalized cues that help them complete tasks autonomously, enhancing dignity and self-efficacy while reducing dependence on staff for routine activities.

Reduces caregiver burden by automating routine prompting and monitoring, allowing staff to focus on higher-level care tasks and meaningful engagement with residents/patients.

Leverages behavioral data to identify patterns and trends, allowing care teams to address potential issues before they escalate and tailor interventions to individual needs more effectively.

Improves compliance with infection prevention protocols through timely reminders and monitoring, helping to minimize the spread of communicable diseases in care environments.
